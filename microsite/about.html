Why does this website exist?

I was upset with Firefox's lack of easy customization of the home page. Yes there's extensions, however, I wanted something that's truly my own. That's why I felt it was necessary to write my own page instead of just learning to write a Firefox theme. It'll be easier right? And it honestly kinda was.

The solution!

Now this website exists, and it's kinda good. I might actually use it as the homepage for my browser. You can do it too, I don't really care. Should you have your own custom home page? I dunno, do you want to spend time writing it? Do I want to spend time writing this monologue? Why do we do anything? Why do we meet anyone? What is life? Someone help me figure out this conundrum! Or should I? I don't know, I'm just a website!
